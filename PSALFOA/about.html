The Public School Athletic League Football Officials Association, Inc. (PSALFOA) was organized in 1961 in Brooklyn, New York. The organization was created to train and provide certified football officials for the New York City Department of Education. The PSALFOA is the most recent chapter to join the New York State Association of Certified Football Officials, Inc. (NYSACFO), the governing body for football officiating in New York State. When the PSALFOA joined the NYSACFO in 1981, it completed the standardization of football officiating throughout New York State. At that time we had been using college rules (NCAA) to call our games. We transitioned to National Federation of State High School Association (NFHS) rules and officiating mechanics. The NFHS is the governing body for high school sports throughout the country.
